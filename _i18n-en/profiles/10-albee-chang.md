# Albee Chang

## Albee Chang

Translating the Power of Goodness Through Media｜Visual Creation × Sustainability Narrative × Cross-Media Planning

Co-Founder, Zhuose Image Studio

SDGs: 4, 11, 12, 17

## I. A Practitioner of Media and Goodness

Albee Chang is a practitioner who hopes to bring a little more goodness to the world through the translating power of media. She has taken part in a wide range of creative planning across copywriting, artistic creativity, and sustainable living: short films, 4A advertising, music videos, television programs, and podcasts are all within her field of practice.

At the core of these works, each begins from a starting point of sustainability, giving her creations both depth and breadth.

## II. Zhuose Image Studio

Established for nearly a decade, Zhuose Image Studio is made up of several partners with a deep passion for visual imagery. They fulfill their clients' visions with sincerity, and in recent years have integrated the core principles of the SDGs to help industries across sectors plan innovative visual works and experimental media.

## III. Her Intention in Joining the Club

Albee believes that every encounter and every deep conversation can open up a more fitting translation and expression for the lives of all involved. For this reason, she joined the Rotary Club of Taipei Sustainable Impact, hoping to co-create the impact of goodness together with everyone, and to contribute her own effort to this clamorous era.
