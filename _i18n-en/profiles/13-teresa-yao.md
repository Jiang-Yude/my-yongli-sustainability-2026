# S. Teresa Yao

**Satellite Club Secretary · Secretary-General, Taiwan Old Mother Hen Association**

## S. Teresa Yao

A Force Accompanying Rural Nepal Toward Sustainability｜Education Empowerment × Women's Care × Local Sustainability

Secretary-General, Taiwan Old Mother Hen Association (incorporated association)

SDGs: 1, 4, 5, 17

## I. A Local Force Rooted in Nepal

Ms. Teresa Yao is the Executive Director of the Taiwan Old Mother Hen Association. She has long worked deeply in Nepal, devoting herself through concrete action to education, women's empowerment, and care for the disadvantaged. One of her greatest achievements is the successful establishment of a local NGO in Nepal, gradually cultivating local talent and integrating local resources so that services can operate sustainably and become a genuine local force.

## II. Accompaniment Is the Most Authentic Form of Aid

She believes that truly valuable international aid lies in accompanying local people as they grow, giving communities the capacity to change their own future. For this reason, over many years she has devoted herself to building cross-cultural cooperation platforms, connecting schools, communities, volunteers, and professionals to lay a lasting foundation of service for rural Nepal.

## III. Her Hopes in Joining the Club

In joining the Club, she hopes to make more like-minded friends and to help more people come to know the efforts and good works of the Taiwan Old Mother Hen Association in Nepal. She also looks forward to gathering more warmth and strength through exchange and cooperation, working together to bring positive change to the world.
