## 🟡 建議修（語域/自然度/文法）
1.  **[index.md] 頁尾製作資訊略顯生硬。**
    *   原文：「Knowledge architecture and web design: Jiang Yu-de」
    *   建議：「Information architecture and web design by Jiang Yu-de」 (Information architecture 更常用，加上 by 使語句更完整)

2.  **[about-yongli.md] 「四個信念」的翻譯較為直白。**
    *   原文：「The report is a doorway. The knowledge base is the long-term value.」
    *   建議：「The report serves as an introduction, while the knowledge base provides long-term value.」(語氣更正式，符合報告書風格)

3.  **[analysis.md] 標題可以更自然。**
    *   原文：「The Club Is a Healthy Dual Track of 'Heavy on Doing + With Institutions'」
    *   建議：「Structural Insight: A Healthy Dual Track of Action and Institutional Support」(更符合分析報告的標題風格)

4.  **[cases.md] 敘述可以更流暢。**
    *   原文：「The content is written based on the on-site statistics from Xingang on April 20, 2026, and the review text.」
    *   建議：「This case study is based on statistics and post-event reviews from the service provided at Xingang on April 20, 2026.」("review text" 語意不清，post-event review 更準確)

5.  **[methodology.md] 結語的措辭可以更精練。**
    *   原文：「Writing out 'areas that could be better next' on our own initiative is, in itself, this report's demand on us. It lets readers know we are continually adjusting, and it gives our future selves directions that can be tracked and compared.」
    *   建議：「Proactively identifying 'areas for future improvement' is a core part of our commitment to accountability. This practice ensures continuous adjustment and provides a clear roadmap for future comparison and progress tracking.」(更緊湊，使用 demand on us 稍嫌強烈，commitment to accountability 更貼切)

6.  **[profiles/01-cp-lin.md] 標題與內文中的動詞形式。**
    *   原文：「Builder of Value-Driven Spaces: advancing "doing good" into an impact system that can operate over the long term」
    *   建議：「Builder of Value-Driven Spaces: Advancing "Doing Good" into a Sustainable Impact System」(句首動詞大寫，"operate over the long term" 可簡化為 "sustainable")

7.  **[profiles/07-nick-lin.md] 對永續的定義可以更優雅。**
    *   原文：「Sustainability is letting good value endure over the long term.」
    *   建議：「Sustainability means ensuring that positive values endure over the long term.」(letting 稍嫌口語，ensuring 更正式)

8.  **[services/06-community.md] 計畫摘要的動詞時態。**
    *   原文：「This project uses the SROI (Social Return on Investment) model to evaluate the effectiveness of its inputs, with the following long-term impacts expected:」
    *   建議：「This project will use the SROI... model to evaluate..., with the following long-term impacts anticipated:」 (由於是計畫書，使用未來式 will use 更貼切，anticipated 比 expected 稍正式)

## 🟢 一致性與細節
1.  **[多個 profile 檔案] 人物誌標題格式可以統一。**
    *   問題：在 `profiles/01-cp-lin.md` 中，標題是「CP Impact」，下方才是副標題。但在 `profiles/02-vicky-lee.md` 中，直接是「Vicky Lee」，下方是副標題。
    *   建議：統一格式。例如，都使用「姓名 (扶輪稱謂)」作為主標題。`Lin Hsueh-hsien (CP Impact)`，`Lee Chi (Vicky)` 等。目前稿件已接近此格式，僅需微調確保完全一致。

2.  **[多個檔案] "Member" vs. "Club Member"**
    *   觀察：多數地方使用 "Member"，但在 `members.md` 中有「5 officers, 12 Club Members」。
    *   建議：雖然兩者都可，但全文統一使用 "Member" 即可，因為上下文已清楚指涉是社內成員。可將 "12 Club Members" 改為 "12 members"。

3.  **[多個檔案] "the Club" 的指涉。**
    *   觀察：在各個檔案中，"the Club" 被用來指代「永力社」，這在長文脈絡中是好的做法。
    *   建議：在文件或章節的開頭，應先出現一次全名 `Rotary Club of Taipei Sustainable Impact`，後續再用 "the Club" 指代，以確保新讀者能清楚理解。目前稿件大部分都做到了，僅需確認每個獨立頁面開頭是否清晰。
