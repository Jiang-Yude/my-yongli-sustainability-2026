# How to Build Your Personal Page

For Club Members

Talk with the AI drafting assistant for **15 minutes**, chatting about your Chinese name, job title, sustainability theme, and social media links. At the end, **copy the whole conversation result and paste it into a single form**, and you are done.

It is fine if you are not used to AI. The whole process is just like chatting with a person.

- See the process first
- Start using the Drafting Assistant now
- Open the Club Form now

## 01. Why Does This Exist?

In the Club's annual Sustainability Impact Report, every Member will have a **personal page** (like the existing ones for Vicky, Albee, and Wayne). But collecting information often runs into three problems:

### Pain Point 1: Not Knowing What to Provide

"Name, job title, bio, photo, social media, SDGs..." Listing too much at once makes people want to put it off.

### Pain Point 2: Repeated Back-and-Forth

First the name is given, then we go back and ask for the job title, then the bio, then the photo. Each time a little more is added, and it never seems to end.

### Pain Point 3: Formats That Do Not Match

For the same "one-sentence theme," each person writes it very differently, and in the end we still have to spend time polishing it again.

So we designed a **Gemini drafting assistant**. It will organize your information through a question-and-answer exchange, and produce a file in a unified format at the end. **You do not have to think about the format or the order**. Just chat with it.

## 02. What Kind of Personal Page Will You Get?

This round we are first doing a **text-only version**, with the goal of getting every Member online. Advanced versions with photos, impact stories, and case collections will be opened up later, with a separate notice at that time.

### This Round: Text-Only Card Version

Name, job title, sustainability theme, and social media links, presented cleanly as text only. First make sure everyone has a page, without getting stuck on materials.

**You need to prepare**: Chinese name, English name, company job title, a one-sentence theme, a three-part bio, SDGs, and at least 1 social media link.

### Next Round: Add a Profile Photo

After the text-only version has been online for a while, we will collectively open up the option to add a profile photo (a front-facing solo photo of 300x300 or larger).

**Current status**: Photos are not being collected for now. The coordination contact will give separate notice of the submission specifications in the next round.

### Future: Dynamic Advanced Version

Optional add-ons such as an impact map, multiple roles, video, and a case collection. You can refer to the current full version for Vicky.

**Current status**: Not yet available. Decide whether to upgrade after seeing your own text-only version first.

## 03. How to Start? Four Steps

Here we designed an "AI drafting assistant." You simply chat with it to get your information organized.

1. **Open the Drafting Assistant**: Click the button at the bottom of this page to open the Gemini conversation.
2. **Guided Data Entry**: The assistant will ask one question at a time, and give examples to help you if there is anything you are unsure how to write.
3. **Copy the Whole Thing**: When the conversation ends, the assistant will compile a complete passage.
4. **Paste into the Form**: Open the form and paste the content into the single field.

- **How long it takes**: The basic version takes about **15-20 minutes**. When you get stuck, the assistant will give examples for reference, so you do not have to come up with things from scratch.
- **Not used to Gemini?**: It is much like chatting on LINE. As long as you can input Chinese, you are fine. The whole process is in Traditional Chinese.

## 04. What to Prepare Before You Start?

You do not need to have everything ready. **"Write down what you can think of, and the assistant will guide you through what you cannot."** The table below covers the content the assistant will chat with you about.

### Basic Information (Required)

- Chinese name (e.g., Zhang Aibi)
- English name / Rotary name (e.g., Albee)
- Company name
- Job title at the company
- Position in the Club (Club President / committee chair / general Member, etc.)

### Sustainability Theme (Required)

- A one-sentence introduction of yourself (10-20 characters; the assistant will help polish it)
- Three keywords (e.g., visual creation x sustainability narrative x cross-media curation)
- A three-part bio (80-150 characters each: profession / company / the intention behind joining the Club)
- Corresponding SDG numbers (3-4, chosen from the 17 goals)

(Not sure how to choose SDGs? The assistant will list all 17 goals for you to check off.)

### Social Media Links (At Least 1)

- Official website / company website
- Facebook (personal page or fan page)
- Instagram
- LinkedIn / Threads / YouTube / official LINE

(Any link that can be shared publicly will do, and the more the better.)

### Profile Photo (Not Collected This Round)

- This round is text-only, so no photo is needed
- The next round will collectively open up the option to add a profile photo
- At that time, the coordination contact will give separate notice of the specifications and submission method

(Just complete and publish the text-only version first, and add the photo later all at once.)

## 05. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I do not know how to use AI or Gemini. What should I do?**

Think of it as chatting with someone on LINE. As long as you can input Traditional Chinese, you are fine. The assistant will proactively ask you questions, and you just answer truthfully. If you are unsure how to respond, ask the assistant to give you an example.

**Q: Do I have to fill in everything?**

No. **This round is text-only first.** Photos and impact stories are not collected. Just chat about your name, job title, one-sentence theme, bio, and social media links. Future material upgrades will be invited in phases, so it will not pile up all at once.

**Q: Will what I write be changed without my approval?**

Before polishing, the assistant will show you first. **It is only written into the final version after you nod.** During the conversation, you can say "do not change it this way" or "use the original" at any time, and it will listen. The content compiled at the end of the conversation will also be shown to you first. You only copy and paste it into the form once you are satisfied.

**Q: What if I have to leave halfway through?**

Gemini keeps the conversation record, so you can pick up where you left off the next time you open it. Alternatively, you can ask the assistant to "summarize my current progress," copy and save the partial content, and paste it back next time.

**Q: I see others have photos and complete stories. Will I lose out if this round does not collect them?**

No. **This round has the goal of "everyone having a page first."** The text-only version is just as official. The next round will collectively open up the option to add photos, and those who later want to do a full impact-story version (like Vicky) will be invited separately. Build the foundation first, and there will be things to add later.

**Q: Who is the data sent to? Where does it go?**

After the conversation ends, please **copy the whole thing into the Club's Google Form** (see the button below for the link). Once the form is received, AI will automatically interpret the content, apply the layout, and first put it in the proofreading area (G site) for your confirmation. After proofreading passes, it will be pushed to the official website (3481rctsi.vercel.app).

## 06. Start Now

### Ready?

First open the "Drafting Assistant" for a chat, then copy the whole thing when done and paste it into the "Club Form," and you are finished.

1. Open the Drafting Assistant
2. Open the Club Form

(Both buttons open in a new tab. Please press 1 first, finish the conversation, then press 2 to paste the content into the single field of the form.)

After the form is received, AI will build your personal page within about 1-3 days and first place it in the proofreading area. Once the proofreading-area URL is confirmed, the coordination contact will proactively notify you to come back and review. Once you have reviewed it with no issues, it will be pushed to the official website.
