I. A Technology Practitioner in Cross-Disciplinary R&D

Eric Cheng, Associate Manager at Jing-Jun Technology, has long been engaged in opto-mechatronics integration, automation equipment, and precision control technology development, with many years of cross-disciplinary R&D and hands-on industry experience. He has previously taken part in academic research and entrepreneurial ventures, and continues to advance the industrial application of mechanical engineering technology.

II. Jing-Jun Technology: A Path to Self-Reliance from Technology to Market

At Jing-Jun Technology, he continues to advance the R&D and application of automation equipment and precision control systems. Combining engineering technology with industry practice, he actively expands markets for technology applications and promotes the development of self-reliance in domestically produced equipment.

III. His Vision in Joining the Club

Eric Cheng joined the Club hoping that, through cross-disciplinary exchange, he could combine industrial and innovative energies, build cooperative relationships with more outstanding entrepreneurs and professionals, and work together to advance the upgrading of Taiwan's technology industry. Through the sharing of experience and the integration of resources, he aims to create more competitive innovative results.
